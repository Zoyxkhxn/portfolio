Plant Care Assistant

Developed a user-friendly E-commerce platform tailored for plant enthusiasts, leveraging technologies like HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, PHP, and MySQL. The platform enables users to register, log in, and explore a diverse selection of plants. Users can effortlessly add plants to their cart, make secure purchases, and receive personalized plant care tips based on their geographic location. Additionally, the platform offers a unique feature allowing users to customize their virtual garden, enhancing the shopping experience and helping them plan and visualize their garden layouts. This project demonstrates a seamless integration of e-commerce functionality with practical gardening advice, catering to both novice and experienced gardeners.
